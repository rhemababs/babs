Thousands of years ago, an evil robot named Lyric the Last Ancient used a crystal with destructive power to take over the world. His plan was quickly suppressed by the ancients, who destroyed the crystal and sealed the name Lyric. But now he has broken the seal and searches for the crystal fragments to continue his conspiracy once again, in the process of implementing his plot Lyric also captured and controlled Shadow’s mind and Amy made them minions for themselves. Controlling Sonic, Tails, Knuckles and Stick, your task is to find and defeat Lyric as well as rescue your friends. With the familiar side-scrolling platform gameplay, you will have to overcome many complex terrains, avoid pitfalls and defeat the enemies in the way. During the game, you can also switch between four characters at any time, each with their own fighting abilities and styles. Try to collect the crystals because they will bring upgrades that make the character stronger.
